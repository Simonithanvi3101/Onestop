        The game is very well put together with amazing graphics and fun to be had. Need for Speed offers some unique races, a huge open map and great multiplayer. However, the racing is short lived as you find yourself doing the same thing over and over again. Also, the integrated constant online feature messes with your single player - as you are racing AI, online drivers could drive into you whilst free roaming, this then messes with your race. Overall, for the price - it is worth it. However, the fun will not last unless you start finding your own challenges. There are only 50 cars in the game which is terrible, but the game focuses on customisation, so this is understandable. Graphics are stunning (other than the fact it is always night) the story isn't very in depth but the multiplayer is very good. Buy it if you love the franchise, if you want to play with friends or you don't get bored easily. The game is good for street car lovers. Less
